# FAQ

### Is IRPF safe for Online?
Yes — because IRPF **does not support Online usage** at all.  
It is single-player only.

### Will IRPF support GTA VI / RPF8?
Yes — The experimental backend are designed around future RPF formats.

### Is IRPF open-source?
Yes — under the IMFL license.

### Can I build plugins?
Yes! IRPF has a full plugin architecture.

### Can IRPF export DLC for FiveM?
Yes — IRPF includes multi-destination support.
